# SDS-COOL-09 — Spindle Coolant Handling & Lockout Requirements

## Section 1 — Identification
Product: Fabrikam SC-7 synthetic spindle coolant concentrate (water-miscible).
Use: through-spindle and flood cooling on FPX-series machining centers.

## Section 2 — Hazard identification
Concentrate is mildly alkaline (pH 9.2). May cause skin and eye irritation.
Diluted working solution (5–8%) is low hazard but can harbor bacteria if not
maintained.

## Section 4 — First aid
- Eyes: rinse 15 minutes with water; seek medical attention if irritation
  persists.
- Skin: wash with soap and water; apply barrier cream for repeated exposure.

## Section 6 — Accidental release
Contain spills with absorbent. Diluted coolant is slippery — clean immediately to
prevent fall hazards around the FPX-2000.

## Lockout / tagout (LOTO)
Before any coolant or chiller service (see MNT-COOL-12), de-energize and lock out
the main disconnect. Verify zero energy at the spindle drive. Hot coolant can
exceed 60 °C; allow to cool below 30 °C before opening the chiller enclosure.

## Section 7 — Storage and maintenance
Store concentrate between 5 °C and 35 °C; protect from freezing. Maintain working
solution concentration with a refractometer and monitor pH weekly. Replace
coolant when pH drops below 8.5 or bacterial count is high.

## Section 8 — Personal protection
Nitrile gloves and safety glasses when handling concentrate. Provide mist
extraction at the FPX-2000 enclosure; airborne coolant mist limit 0.5 mg/m³.
