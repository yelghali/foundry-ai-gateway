# SDS-HYD-07 — Hydraulic Fluid Safety Data Sheet

## Section 1 — Identification
Product: Fabrikam HF-32 mineral hydraulic oil and HF-WG water-glycol fluid.
Use: industrial hydraulic power transmission. Supplier emergency line:
+1-800-555-0142.

## Section 2 — Hazard identification
- Mineral oil: not classified as acutely toxic. Prolonged skin contact may cause
  dermatitis. Mist is combustible above the flash point (215 °C).
- Water-glycol (HF-WG): contains ethylene glycol; harmful if swallowed. Keep away
  from food areas.

## Section 4 — First aid
- Skin: wash with soap and water. Remove contaminated clothing.
- Eyes: rinse 15 minutes with water; seek medical attention for HF-WG.
- Ingestion (HF-WG): do not induce vomiting; seek immediate medical care.

## Section 7 — Handling and storage
Store between 5 °C and 40 °C, away from oxidizers. Do not mix mineral and
water-glycol fluids — cross-contamination degrades both. When converting an
HPX-450 from mineral to water-glycol, install the -WG seal kit and flush per
MNT-PUMP-21 before refilling.

## Section 8 — Exposure controls
Wear nitrile gloves and safety glasses. Provide ventilation where oil mist may
form. Occupational exposure limit for oil mist: 5 mg/m³ (8-hour TWA).

## Section 13 — Disposal
Dispose of used fluid as regulated industrial waste per local regulations. Do not
discharge to drains. Water-glycol fluids require licensed hazardous-waste
handling.
